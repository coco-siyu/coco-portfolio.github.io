In this practice, I would like to research if there is any association between continent and the debt figure so I chose this design. In addition, I am curious to see which continent has the most data. Hence, I craeted 5 groups under 'Location' to color-code countries and dragged the 'value' to the mask card. This visualization presents a bubble chart that coloar coded each country based on its continent. Each bubble's size is based on the value of the debt. Differences between the bubble chart and the heatmap:

  • the actual debt values are not presented in the bubble chart but the size of the bubbles.
  • each country is color coded based on its continent.
